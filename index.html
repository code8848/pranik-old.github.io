About Me

As a graduate student at Texas A&M University, I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Civil Engineering with a specialization in Transportation. Along with my degree, I possess a diverse set of skills and experiences in the transportation industry. I have a keen interest in data science and enjoy working with large datasets.
