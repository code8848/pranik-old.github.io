About Me

As a graduate student at Texas A&M University, I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Civil Engineering with a specialization in Transportation. Along with my degree, I possess a diverse set of skills and experiences in the transportation industry. I have a keen interest in data science and enjoy working with large datasets. In my work, I have experience with tasks such as handling millions of data points, preprocessing and cleaning data, conducting Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), building models, and visualizing results. Additionally, I am comfortable collaborating with multiple team members and utilizing git for code sharing. I adhere to AGILE principles for effective project management and collaboration.
